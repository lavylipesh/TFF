About Chago

Chago is a storage facility that allows farmers to store their cereals in a safe space.

Our storage involves putting cereal in airtight plastic containers to avoid contamination of the products.

We descided to start the project after a number of people complained about aflotoxins in their grain during the past years.

We came up with the idea of storing cereals in airtight containers after many cases of aflotoxins in grain had been reported around the country.

We are a team of four enthusiastic kenyan students who have a great passsion in farm produce.

Our idea is to use plastic containers for storage because they are airtight.

Plastic containers are also scalable, cheap and easily available.
